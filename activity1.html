Introduction

Hello, my name is Monkey D. Luffy, but you can just call me Luffy.

I’m from Foosha Village and I’m 19 years ol. My birthday is on May 05, 2000 , nd I am a pirate aiming to become the King of the Pirates.
I sail on the Thousand Sunny with my crew, the Straw Hat Pirates.

My mother’s name is unknown, and my father’s name is Monkey D. Dragon. I have one brother, and his name is Portgas D. Ace.
My hobbies include eating meat, adventuring across the seas, and testing my rubber powers. Most of the time, I spend searching for treasure and fighting strong opponents.
My dream is to find the One Piece and become the King of the Pirates.

“I don’t want to conquer anything. I just think the guy with the most freedom in this whole ocean… is the Pirate King!”
- Monkey D. Luffy
